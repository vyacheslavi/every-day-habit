How it works

Start forming your habits with EveryDayHabits. Healthy habits will improve your life in the long run. Be realistic and don't set too much limits, otherwise you'll just drop the case.

To get started:

  1. Click on "+ new habit" to create a habit. Set a name and goal for this habit. In the future, you can change these parameters.
  2. Click on "+ note" to write a note. In the future, you can change date and text of note too.
  3. Click on the cell in the table to mark the fulfillment of today's habit. Click again to remove the mark.
